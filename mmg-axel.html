5. Bazaar

Bazaar Extended Statistics

When you dive into using buy and sell orders on the Bazaar, you'll quickly notice a common problem: players frequently outbid or undercut your orders almost instantly. Leave your listings unattended for too long, and you risk waiting ages for them to fill. To work around this, it‚Äôs crucial to assess the item turnover first.
